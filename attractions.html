Attractions

Beaches
Beaches encircle the island and you can find public or private beaches to enjoy.
Rainforest
Be very careful as the rainforest is a natural wildlife habitat. Birds and other animals will cross your path.
Volcano
An active volcano once eruped and is now in wait.
History Museum
Check out what happened in the past and learn about the culture.
Fishing Tours
Love to get away on a boat for the day? Go explore Tanitis fish, a perfect tropical ocean trip.
Snorkeling
Explore deep into the depths of the coral reef and find new fish lurking!
Zip-lining
From one tree to another, you too can zip around in the rainforest, just like the birds.
Pubs
There are many places to have a drink! There is even a microbrewery!
Dance Club
Can't get away from the music? Stay in the city for the night and dance your heart away!
Movie Theater
Family always loves to get together and watch new thrilling shows! A cinema opens your mind to new things!
Helicopter Rides
What's better than seeing the whole island at once? Here you can truly take in the tropical paradise.
Arcade
Are your kids bored? Take them to the arcade where there will never be a sad time again!
Art Galleries
Art lovers around will come here just for the gallery. An open room with many pieces from artists on the island.
Bowling
If you can't go a week without throwing a ball at pins, then wait no longer!
Golf Course
A nine-hole golf course should be operational by next year.
Sightseeing
Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, from native architecture to sandy beaches.
